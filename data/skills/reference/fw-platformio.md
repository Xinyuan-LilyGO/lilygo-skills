# Framework: PlatformIO

Use for PlatformIO Core, `platformio.ini`, `pio run`, upload, and monitor prompts.

- Primary authority: official PlatformIO Core documentation.
- Treat PlatformIO as a runner around Arduino-ESP32 or ESP-IDF projects.
- Pair upload and monitor prompts with `debug-flash-serial`.
- Do not claim a PlatformIO environment works without command output.
